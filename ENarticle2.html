Jennifer Lawrence, actress and feminist : a life like no other.

At only 21 years old, the American actress Jennifer Lawrence has become a well-known star. Originally from Kentucky in the United States, Jennifer Lawrence is an actress born on August 15, 1990 who became famous mainly throughout the trilogy Hunger Games. Since then, her fame has continued to grow.

Jennifer Lawrence.

In fact, in 2014, she received at the Golden Globes ceremony, the trophy for the best second role in her performance on American Bluff. Nominated for the Academy Awards for Best Supporting Actress, the actress goes through so much casting that will subsequently allow her to gain international exposure.

In 2014 she got the role of Raven in the famous blockbuster X-Men: Days of Future. By her courage and determination, Jennifer Lawrence is among the hundred most influential people in the world according to Time magazine and is named the most powerful woman in the entertainment industry. Lastly, she became the second highest paid actress in Hollywood.

Involvement in feminism.

Over the years, she has also placed her fame at the service of her convictions. In fact, Jennifer Lawrence took the opportunity to convey certain ideals that she feels are important to point out. She remains very involved in the fight for women's rights.

She also defends the pro-choice abortion movement. After being the victim in 2014 of a leak of intimate photos by a hacker, Jennifer Lawrence denounces the misdeeds known in the film industry. She spoke out against the unfairness that weighs on women’s shoulders. She also pointed out salary inequalities and sexual harassment in the film industry.

Worldwide controversies.

One of the most talked about cases in the film industry will most likely be the Harvey Weinstein case. This case remains the result of sexual harassment and assault committed by Harvey Weinstein, an American film producer known worldwide.

Arrest of Harvey Weinstein.

On October 5, 2017, more than 80 women accused him of rape or sexual assault. Since then, Harvey Weinstein was fired from his company and many investigations found him guilty.

On March 11, 2020, Harvey was sentenced to 23 years in prison. This case highlited many debate about violence against women. The status of women in the film industry was also being brought up.

An optimistic point of view.

Even though this case has been the center of the world, Jennifer Lawrence keeps saying that being a woman in the film industry is not awful.

During the shooting of Hunger Games, she mentioned : "We have the ability to control the image that young girls are going to perceive in the film. Girls see enough bodies that they will never be able to obtain. It's an amazing opportunity to rid ourselves of that in this industry." I guess Jennifer Lawrence is all about women empowerment !

She also said that "women can still be strong and remain sensitive and empathetic”.At the age of 25, Jennifer Lawrence has become the highest paid actress in the world, earning $52 million in one year.

Through her actions and convictions, Jennifer Lawrence is making a name for herself on the international field and is reflecting the achievements made by wellknown feminist.
