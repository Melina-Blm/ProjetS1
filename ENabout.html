My name is Mélina BOUCHELLEGHEM and I am 18 years old. For my first year in the Multimedia and Internet professions in Montbéliard, I had the objective to create a website in order to succeed in my individual project.

The exercise consisted in creating a site highlighting the issue of feminism through 3 articles.

Being strongly passionate about the world of cinema, I decided to look into this subject and to link it with feminism. I mentioned the movie Hunger Games with the actress Jennifer Lawrence. This allowed me to take out the negative sides for women in the movie industry.


I hope the site has been useful to you.
