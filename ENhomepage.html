A BITTER TRUTH

It took quite some time for women to assert themselves in the movie industry. Time,strength, and a movement : feminism!

Unfortunately, even if women are still less represented than men in the cinema nowadays, it is by following its evolution/path over the years that we realize how much progress has been made.

The film industry has long adhered to the stereotypes that invaded women, leading to a movement: feminism.

The actress Jennifer Lawrence has led a fight for feminism by denouncing the inequalities present in the film industry.

By its story, Hunger Games constitutes a transition phase in the representation of women on screen.
